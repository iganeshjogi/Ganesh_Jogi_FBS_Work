JOINS QUERIES:

Q1) Display Employee Name and Job Title.

Ans:

mysql> select concat(e.first_name,' ',e.last_name) as 'Employee Name', j.job_title as 'Job Title'
    -> from employees as e inner join jobs as j
    -> on e.job_id = j.job_id ;

Q49) Display employees working under the same manager.

Ans: 

select e1.employee_name as Employee1,
       e2.employee_name as Employee2,
       e1.manager_id
from employee_manager e1
inner JOIN employee_manager e2
on e1.manager_id = e2.manager_id
where e1.employee_id < e2.employee_id;


Q50) Display manager names who manage more than 2 employees.

Ans:
select m.employee_name as 'Manager Name',
       count(e.employee_id) as 'Total Employees'
from employee_manager m
inner join employee_manager e
on m.employee_id = e.manager_id
group by m.employee_name
having count(e.employee_id) > 2;
